Return Policy

We want you to love your purchase! If you're not completely satisfied, here's what you need to know about returns.

15-Day Return Window

You have 15 days from the date of purchase to return items. The item must be in its original condition with tags attached.

Original Condition Required

Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging. All tags must be attached and intact.

Proof of Purchase

A valid receipt or proof of purchase is required for all returns. Keep your receipt safe!

Non-Returnable Items

Undergarments, swimwear, and discounted sale items cannot be returned for hygiene and policy reasons.

Return Process

1

Visit Our Store

Bring your item and receipt to any FOG Fashion location

2

Item Inspection

Our team will verify the condition and authenticity

3

Return Processed

Choose store credit, exchange, or refund (conditions apply)

Refund Policy

Understanding our refund process and what to expect when returning items.

Card Payments

Refunds to credit/debit cards take 5-7 business days to appear in your account after processing.

Cash Payments

Cash refunds are available immediately at the store for purchases made with cash.

Store Credit

Store credit is issued immediately and never expires. Can be used at any FOG Fashion location.

Refund Conditions

  • Full refund available within 15 days with receipt
  • Store credit offered for items beyond 15 days (up to 30 days)
  • Defective items eligible for full refund regardless of time
  • Sale items (50% off or more) - store credit only
  • Items without receipt - store credit at current price only
  • Damaged or worn items not eligible for refund
